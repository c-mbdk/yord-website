What is YORD?

CFT Youth of Royal Destiny is a youth group striving to bring out the best in every youth in a Godly and loving environment. We believe every youth is gifted by God and can contribute positively first to their community, then to the world. Each year, we hold a youth conference, focused on empowering and enabling youth towards excellence. We're passionate about building transformational leaders for today and the future generation.
